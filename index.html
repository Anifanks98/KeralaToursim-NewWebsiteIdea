THINGS TO DO

Ayurveda

Ayurveda has been nurtured on these shores for over a thousand years and the world is slowly awakening to the practical applications of this ancient form of medicine. Ayurveda focuses on the overall holistic growth and well-being of the individual.

Scuba Diving

Discover the magical underwater world with Bond Safari Kovalam. All age groups can take part in this exciting aquatic experience. No diving or scuba experience is needed.

Paragliding

Soar through the sky at amazing altitudes, enjoying the pleasure of being able to fly freely through the air. Paragliding, one of the most amazing adventure sports in the world, is right here in Kerala to take you to new heights of joy and entertainment.
